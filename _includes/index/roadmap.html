Roadmap

  • August 2021

    The Idea was Born

    The idea was born from a dream of direct democracy: a world where people have more trust in each other and feel more in control of their immediate environment; A place where people help each other, by simply sharing what we already know without compromising our social growth.

  • December 2022

    Minimum Viable Product Specification

    The specification for the Minimum Viable Product is complete and we have started building it! The MVP platform only has volunteers and users; the sponsors will be added in the full version.

  • December 2023

    MVP Launch

    We are expecting to launch the MVP version of the application in Bristol (UK) and Nis (Serbia)!

  • January 2024

    Phase Two Expansion

    After the MVP launch we'll just keep working until the full platform version is up and running.

Roadmap

  • Q3 2021

    The Idea was Born

    The idea was born from a dream of direct democracy. A world where people have more trust in each other and feel more in control over their immediate environment. A place where we can help each other by simply sharing what we already know but also have direct control over our social budget.

  • Q1 2022

    Minimum Viable Product Specification

    The specification for the Minimum Viable Product is complete and we have started building it! The MVP platform only has volunteers and users, the sponsors will be added in the full version.

  • Q4 2022

    MVP Launch

    We are expecting to launch the MVP version of the application in Bristol (UK) and Nis (Serbia)!

  • Q1 2023

    Phase Two Expansion

    Straight after the MVP launch we will carry on the work on the full platform version.
